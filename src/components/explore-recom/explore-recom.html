Recommendation

The Revenant(2015)

Director: Alejandro González Iñárritu

Writers: Mark L. Smith, Alejandro González Iñárritu

In 1823, Hugh Glass guides Andrew Henry’s trappers through unorganized territory. While he and his half-Pawnee son, Hawk, are hunting, the company's camp is attacked by an Arikara war party. Guided by Glass, the survivors travel on foot to Fort Kiowa, as traveling downriver will make them vulnerable. Glass fights for survival after being mauled badly by a grizly bear and left for dead by members of his own hunting team. Unexpectedly, Glass actually miraculously survived. After this painful wild experience, he began his revenge plan.
