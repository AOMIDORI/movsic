Best Soundtrack of 2017

The Revenant(2015)

Director: Alejandro González Iñárritu

Writers: Mark L. Smith, Alejandro González Iñárritu

Awards: The Revenant Wins 3 2016 Oscars. The Revenant was awarded three Oscars at the 88th Academy Awards, including the first-ever win for actor Leonardo DiCaprio, who'd received five previous nominations.

Reason: As one of the main actors in The Revenant, Leonardo DiCaprio performed very well in the movie and won about 10 rewards such as Academy Award for Best Actor and Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture – Drama.

Description: In 1823, Hugh Glass guides Andrew Henry’s trappers through unorganized territory. While he and his half-Pawnee son, Hawk, are hunting, the company's camp is attacked by an Arikara war party. Guided by Glass, the survivors travel on foot to Fort Kiowa, as traveling downriver will make them vulnerable. Glass fights for survival after being mauled badly by a grizly bear and left for dead by members of his own hunting team. Unexpectedly, Glass actually miraculously survived. After this painful wild experience, he began his revenge plan.
